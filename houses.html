Let's Discover the Hogwarts Houses!

Which one would you choose?

Gryffindor

Founded by Godric Gryffindor, this house values bravery, courage, and chivalry. Its members are daring and bold. If you love adventure and standing up for what's right, Gryffindor is for you.

Slytherin

Founded by Salazar Slytherin, it values ambition, cunning, and resourcefulness. Members are known for their leadership and determination. If you're strategic and driven, Slytherin may be your home.

Hufflepuff

Created by Helga Hufflepuff, this house honors loyalty, patience, and fairness. Hufflepuffs are kind-hearted and hard-working. If you believe in equality and dedication, this house is perfect for you.

Ravenclaw

Founded by Rowena Ravenclaw, this house values intelligence, creativity, and wisdom. If you're a deep thinker who loves learning, Ravenclaw is your ideal match.
